# SoulSync AI Privacy Policy

SoulSync AI collects only the profile, consent, and matching information needed to provide onboarding, persona preview, recommendations, blocking, reporting, and account deletion. The default is **NO training/model-improvement use** of user content, photos, transcripts, embeddings, reports, or recommendations unless a future policy version asks for separate explicit opt-in consent.

## Data Classes

| public | matching_private | internal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Display name, age range, city, public persona summary, approved public photo references, synthetic badge | Matching answers, MBTI/religion/values, interests, city-level location, approved private photo moderation state, embeddings derived only from matching context | Account identifiers, consent ledger, blocks, reports, jobs, audit timestamps, model prompts/outputs needed to operate safety and matching |

## Use

- Public data is used for user-visible profile and recommendation surfaces after safety checks.
- Matching-private data is used to compute compatibility, generate privacy-filtered personas, run simulations, and rank recommendations.
- Internal data is used to secure accounts, honor consent, enforce blocks/reports, operate jobs, and debug safety incidents.

## Sharing

SoulSync AI does not sell personal data. We expose only minimal recommendation summaries and approved, moderated profile assets needed for the product surface. Exact address, workplace, GPS coordinates, and precise home location are not product fields.
